Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our app and get started with confidence.

What devices are compatible with the app? ▽

Our app is optimized for the latest versions of both Android and iOS. For Android, we recommend devices running Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher. For iOS, the app requires iOS 14.0 or later.

  • Android: 8.0+ (most modern devices)
  • iOS: 14.0+ (iPhone 6s and newer)
  • Storage: Minimum 100MB free space
  • Internet: Required for initial setup
Is the app free to download and use? ▽

Yes! The app is completely free to download and use. We offer premium features through optional in-app subscriptions that unlock advanced functionality and remove ads.

  • Free Tier: Core features, limited daily usage
  • Premium: $4.99/month or $49.99/year - Unlimited access, ad-free
  • Family Plan: $7.99/month - Up to 6 accounts
How does the app protect my privacy? ▽

Your privacy is our top priority. We follow strict data protection standards and only collect the minimum information necessary for the app to function properly.

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted
  • No Account Required: Use anonymously without registration
  • Local Storage: Most data stays on your device
  • GDPR Compliant: Full compliance with privacy regulations
  • Clear Permissions: You control what the app can access
What makes this app different from others? ▽

Unlike traditional apps, ours uses advanced AI algorithms and machine learning to provide personalized recommendations and predictive features that adapt to your usage patterns.

  • AI-Powered: Smart recommendations based on your behavior
  • Cross-Platform Sync: Seamless experience across devices
